Pear & chocolate flapjacks

Created by: Cassie Best

Ingredients

  • 150g butter (and a bit more for the tin)
  • 150g light brown soft sugar
  • 4 tbsp yummy honey
  • 300g porridge oats
  • 4 juicy pear halves (from a can)
  • 100g dark chocolate for the top
  • A tiny pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Get Ready: Ask an adult to heat the oven to 200C. Brush your tin with butter and line it with paper.
  2. Melt It: Put butter, sugar, honey, and salt in a pan. Melt them together until bubbly, then let it cool for 5 minutes.
  3. Oat Time: Put your oats in a big bowl.
  4. Chop the Fruit: Drain the pears, dry them with a paper towel, and chop them into small pieces. Add them to the oats.
  5. The Big Stir: Pour the buttery mix over the oats and pears. Stir until everything is sticky and mixed!
  6. Bake: Press the mix into your tin and bake for 25-30 mins until golden.
  7. Chocolate Drizzle: Melt the chocolate in the microwave, then drizzle it all over your treats.
  8. Cool & Eat: Let it cool down, cut into squares, and enjoy!
