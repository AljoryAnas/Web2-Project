Pear & chocolate flapjacks

Created by: Cassie Best
Dessert

Ingredients

  • 150g butter (and a bit more for the tin)
  • 150g light brown soft sugar
  • 4 tbsp yummy honey
  • 300g porridge oats
  • 4 juicy pear halves (from a can)
  • 100g dark chocolate for the top
  • A tiny pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prep time: Ask a grown-up to heat the oven to 200C (180C fan). Use your pastry brush to paint the tin with butter and line it with baking paper.
  2. The Melty Mix: Put the butter, sugar, honey, and salt into a saucepan. Stir it gently over medium heat until it‚Äôs all melted and bubbly. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Oat Power: While the mix cools, weigh your oats into a big mixing bowl.
  4. Pear Prep: Drain the pear juice, pat them dry with kitchen paper, and carefully chop them into small "bite-sized" pieces. Mix them in with the oats.
  5. The Big Mix: Pour the warm, buttery syrup over the oats and pears. Stir it all together until every single oat is coated and shiny!
  6. Bake It: Tip the mix into your tin and press it down flat with the back of your spoon. Bake for 25-30 minutes until it looks like a golden sunset.
  7. Chocolate Magic: Once the flapjacks are out and cooling, melt your chocolate in a small bowl in the microwave (30 seconds, stir, then 15 more).
  8. The Drizzle: Use a spoon to drizzle the chocolate in fun patterns all over the top! Let them cool completely before cutting into 12 yummy squares.
