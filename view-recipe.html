Pear & chocolate flapjacks

Created by: Cassie Best
Dessert

A delicious and healthy treat for kids, combining the sweetness of pears with a hint of chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 200g Rolled Oats
  • 2 Ripe Pears (diced)
  • 50g Dark Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix the oats and pears in a large bowl.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes until golden brown.
